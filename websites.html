My Websites

These are some websites I’ve built — some for myself, others for clients.

Depending on the project’s needs, I’ve developed websites in a variety of ways, from simple static client-side pages (like the one you’re viewing now) to full-stack applications. My full-stack projects have included Python-based backends using frameworks like Django and Flask, and serverless architectures with AWS Lambda functions written in Python.

On the frontend, I’ve primarily worked with Angular to build responsive and user-friendly interfaces. For databases, I often choose MySQL due to its reliability and compatibility with my tech stack.
